WE'RE THE ROCKIN' BOTS

We're FIRST Robotics team 2035, the Robo-Rockin' Bots, from Carmel, California! Our team began in 2007, and has been spreading enthusiasm for STEM throughout the Monterey Peninsula ever since. Every year, we participate in FIRST Robotics Competitions in both Silicon Valley and Sacramento, and our mission is to design innovative robots and promote STEM throughtout our community and others.
